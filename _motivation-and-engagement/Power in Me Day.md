<p>Power in Me Day aims to motivate Southviens to maximise their potential
in learning and achieving their personal best. This day is dedicated for
Southviens to experience and learn school values, social emotional competencies,
practise Growth Mindset and Executive Functioning Skills in authentic situations.
Developing these essential skills and competencies strengthens their self-confidence
and cultivates the resilience needed to tackle life's challenges.</p>
