<p><strong>Objectives</strong>
</p>
<p>The South View Primary School Choir (SV Nightingales) provides students
with the opportunity to discover the joy of singing while developing confidence,
teamwork, and musical skills. Students learn proper vocal techniques, ensemble
skills, and musical expression in a fun and supportive environment. Students
learn values resilience through regular practice, responsibility in preparing
for performances, and respect as they work closely with peers. Through
meaningful musical experiences, the choir helps students enjoy the arts
and build teamwork</p>
<p><strong>Main Activities</strong>
</p>
<p>Pupils will showcase what they have learnt in the following activities:</p>
<p><strong>Weekly Choir Practices</strong> - Focuses on vocal technique, ensemble
singing, listening skills and musicality. Sectionals and small group practices
to strengthen part-singing. Exposure to musical activities</p>
<p><strong>School Events</strong> - opportunities for members to demonstrate
confidence and stage presence</p>
<p><strong>SYF Arts Presentation</strong> - opportunities for members to perform
at the national level</p>
<p><strong>International Choral Competition</strong> - Exposure to high-level
performance standards and broader musical experiences.</p>
<p><strong>Choir Exchanges and Masterclasses</strong> - opportunities for
choristers to sharpen technique and gain inspiration from experienced musicians.</p>
<p><strong>Values-in-action</strong> - encourage students to use their skills
to bring joy to others, reinforce the spirit of service and gratitude</p>
