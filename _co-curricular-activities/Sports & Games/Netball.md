<p><strong>Objectives</strong>
</p>
<p>The Netball CCA aims to build studentsâ€™ interest and confidence in the
sport by strengthening their foundation in key skills, rules, and game
play. Through regular training, students develop discipline, resilience,
and a positive mindset while learning to work as a team and communicate
effectively on court. The programme also nurtures character, sportsmanship,
and emerging leadership, helping students grow into confident and responsible
team players who enjoy the game.</p>
<p><strong>Main Activities</strong>
</p>
<p>Students are divided into two teams, Junior (Primary 4 and 5) and Senior
(Primary 6).</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Under the guidance of a professional and experienced coach, students undergo
training to build their netball techniques, movement abilities, speed,
agility, and overall ball-handling skills.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Students learn fundamental skills such as passing techniques, proper footwork,
catching, and spatial awareness at Primary 4.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Students are taught specialised court positions, team strategies, and
offensive and defensive play structures to prepare them for competitive
matches at Primary 5 and 6.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Through these trainings, students also develop social-emotional competencies
such as self-awareness, relationship management and responsible decision-making.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Students who demonstrate strong netball skills and positive qualities
are given opportunities to participate in intra-team games, friendly matches,

</li>
</ul>
