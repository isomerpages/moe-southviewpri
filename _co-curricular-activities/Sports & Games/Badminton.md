<p><strong>Objectives</strong>
</p>
<p>Badminton CCA aims to provide students with basic skills and knowledge
to play and enjoy badminton. The training also provides opportunities to
shape studentsâ€™ character through responsibility, resilience and self-discipline,
develop their leadership skills, teamwork and sportsmanship.</p>
<p><strong>Main Activities</strong>
</p>
<p>Students are divided into two teams, Boys team and Girls team.</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Under the supervision of professional and experienced coaches, students
are involved in physical training to improve their badminton skills and
fitness levels.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Students are taught basic badminton skills of serving, rallying, smashing
and clearing the net.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Students learn to apply the rules of the game, including the scoring system
for both singles and doubles games.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Students learn to apply game concepts and strategies in their gameplay.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Students who attain the required badminton skills and qualities are invited
to participate in the yearly National School Games.</p>
</li>
</ul>
