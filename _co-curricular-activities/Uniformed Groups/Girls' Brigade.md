<p><strong>Objectives</strong>
</p>
<p>The Girls’ Brigade is a values-driven uniformed group committed to helping
girls grow to their fullest potential through character-building experiences
and meaningful activities. We equip, empower, and enable every Girl to
develop as a confident leader who leads and serves with humility, always
putting others before self.</p>
<p>Guided by the principle of <em>Servant Leadership</em>, we encourage our
Girls to make a positive difference in their communities with confidence,
compassion, and courage.</p>
<p>Key Focus Areas</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Developing leadership skills</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Supporting holistic growth</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Building strong character</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Strengthening skills and knowledge</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Encouraging service and community involvement</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Main Activities</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><strong>Weekly Sessions: </strong>Regular meetings include character-building
lessons,&nbsp;badgework, drills, and games.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Camps and Events:</strong> Members participate in camps and national
events that provide immersive experiences and leadership opportunities.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Badgework: </strong>Girls earn badges by completing a variety
of tasks, encouraging them to learn new skills and grow in different areas.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Leadership Roles:</strong> Members take on responsibilities such
as Squad Leaders, gaining hands-on experience in planning and leading.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Values-in-Action (VIA): </strong>Girls also take part in VIA sessions,
where they engage with and serve the communities in the neighbourhood.</p>
</li>
</ul>
