<p>The Special Education Needs and Support in Education (SENSE) Department
plays a vital role in enhancing the educational experience of our students
with additional needs.
<br>
<br>By fostering a more inclusive and supportive learning environment, SENSE
complements the school's efforts to ensure that every student, regardless
of their individual needs, has the opportunity to thrive holistically,
including personal growth and academic advancement.</p>
