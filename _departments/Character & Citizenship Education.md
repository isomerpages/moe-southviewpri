<p>Total Defence Day</p>
</td>
<td>
<p>Total Defence Day is commemorated on 15 February each year &ndash; the anniversary of the surrender of&nbsp; the British to the Japanese on 15 February 1942. The core message of Total Defence is &ldquo;everyone has a part to play&rdquo; through the six key pillars of Military, Civil, Economic, Social, Digital and Psychological Defence.</p>
<p>Total Defence Day is meant to strengthen our resolve to keep Singapore safe, secure and independent. Every Singaporean should play a part, individually and collectively, to build a strong, secure and cohesive nation. When we are strong, we are able to deal with any crisis.</p>
