<p><strong>Department Vision</strong>
</p>
<p>Every Southvien a confident and competent user of the English Language.</p>
<p><strong>Department Mission</strong>
</p>
<p>To nurture a love for the English Language and develop effective communicators
who read, write, speak and listen with understanding, accuracy and creativity.</p>
<p><strong>Our English Language programmes aim to develop Southviens who:</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>communicate effectively with different audiences, purposes and contexts</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>engage with ideas critically and appreciate multiple perspectives</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>apply what they have learnt to make a positive impact in their community</p>
<p></p>
